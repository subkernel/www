Andigraf Multishot Toy Camera by Woochul Jung

The Andigraf uses 4 various layout lenses, capturing your story in one shot! Just one click to take 4-9 serial shoots and to create images with various effects. It's just like Andy Warhol's pattern-repeating pop art or the multilens LOMO camera.
If you are LOMO fan, you may love this camera.

Sample Photos

Move your finger to change lenses and films.

Camera Features

  • Various shutter speeds and Manual mode
  • GPS Metadata support
  • Front Camera support
  • Flash support
  • Rough Border and Cross Processing

Lenses

  • Uno (1 shot)
  • Coppia (2 shots)
  • Andigraf (4 shots)
  • Quadline (alternative 4 shots)
  • Oktalens (8 shots)
  • Supernova (9 shots)

Films

  • GrafPhoto Color
  • LoMode Color
  • LoMode Redscale
  • Amelie Color
  • Khaosan Color
  • Edie Color
  • GrafProfessional B&W
  • 1945S Classic Sepia
  • Warhola Candy Color
  • Roy Pop Art
